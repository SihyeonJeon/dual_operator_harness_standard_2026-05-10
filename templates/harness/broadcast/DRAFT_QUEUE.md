# Broadcast Draft Queue

This queue tracks external-facing draft artifacts. It is not canonical project
memory. Canonical decisions and task outcomes must be summarized back into
`progress.md`, `session-handoff.md`, `feature_list.json`, `harness/shared/`, or
task artifacts.

| Draft ID | Task/Trace | Type | Title | Source Evidence | Redaction | Approval | Connector | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| draft_UNKNOWN | UNKNOWN | UNKNOWN | UNKNOWN | UNKNOWN | UNKNOWN | NOT_APPROVED | UNKNOWN | EMPTY_QUEUE |

## Status Values

- `EMPTY_QUEUE`
- `DRAFTED`
- `NEEDS_REDACTION`
- `NEEDS_HUMAN_APPROVAL`
- `APPROVED_FOR_PUBLICATION`
- `PUBLISHED`
- `REJECTED`
- `SUPERSEDED`

## Rules

- Publication requires human approval.
- Connector smoke evidence must exist before any real external write.
- Drafts may be generated by SessionEnd/Stop hooks, but publication must not be
  automatic.
